About PlacementorAI

PlacementorAI is a smart and user-friendly placement management system designed to simplify campus recruitment for students, recruiters, and administrators.

The platform ensures a structured and transparent hiring process by clearly separating user roles and responsibilities. Students can explore job opportunities based on their eligibility, recruiters can manage applications efficiently, and admins maintain full control over the system.



Our Mission

Our mission is to make the placement process simple, fair, and efficient for everyone involved, while providing AI-powered guidance to help students prepare better for their careers.



What Makes Us Unique?

  • Role-based system for better security and clarity
  • Only eligible and approved jobs are shown to students
  • Transparent application tracking
  • AI-powered career and interview guidance
  • Clean and easy-to-use interface


How It Works

Students create job applications, recruiters review and update application status, and admins supervise the overall process. This separation ensures there are no conflicts or unauthorized actions within the system.



We aim to continuously improve the platform and make placement management smarter with the help of technology.
